About Us

At Five Al Durub, we dedicate our experience to delivering advanced and integrated solutions that serve industrial, engineering, medical, and training sectors in Saudi Arabia and beyond. We are driven by a practical vision built on quality, precision, and reliability, with a focus on creating real value through specialized metal structures, infrastructure support, facility solutions, and advanced simulation and training systems. We believe our success depends on understanding client needs and translating them into practical high-level solutions that combine technical efficiency, operational flexibility, and commitment to execution standards.

Vision

To be among the leading entities in specialized industrial and engineering solutions and advanced simulation and training systems through a trusted national business entity built on innovation, quality, and readiness to support complex projects in the Kingdom and beyond.

Mission

To provide integrated and reliable solutions in metal fabrication, specialized structures, facility support, and simulation and training systems through specialized capabilities, quality execution, and effective partnerships that create sustainable value for clients and support national directions toward development, localization, and operational excellence.
